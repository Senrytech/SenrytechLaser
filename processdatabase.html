Intelligent Control and Process Database Technology

Intelligent Control and Process Database Technology

With the advancement of Industry 4.0, intelligence has become an important development trend of fiber laser cutting machines. Senrytech Fiber Laser has actively innovated in this regard to provide users with intelligent solutions.

​ Adaptive Cutting Technology: Senrytech Fiber Laser's fiber laser cutting machine is equipped with advanced sensors and intelligent algorithms, which can real-time monitor parameters such as laser power, current, voltage, plasma signals, and workpiece surface status during the cutting process. Through real-time analysis of these parameters, the system can automatically adjust cutting process parameters, such as laser power, cutting speed, gas flow, etc., to adapt to actual situations such as uneven material thickness and differences in surface quality. When cutting a stainless steel plate with slight thickness changes, the system can quickly sense the thickness change and automatically adjust the laser power and cutting speed to ensure stable quality throughout the cutting process and avoid over-cutting or under-cutting.

​ Rich Process Database: The built-in massive process database is a major feature of Senrytech Fiber Laser's fiber laser cutting machine. The database stores the best cutting process parameters for different materials (such as carbon steel, stainless steel, aluminum alloy, copper, etc.) and plates of different thicknesses, including laser power, cutting speed, gas type and flow, focus position, etc. When processing, users only need to input basic information such as material type and thickness, and the system can automatically call the corresponding process parameters without manual debugging, greatly shortening the processing preparation time and reducing the requirements for the skill level of operators. At the same time, users can also optimize and supplement the parameters in the database according to actual processing experience to achieve personalized process customization.​

Remote Monitoring and Diagnosis: With the help of Internet of Things technology, Senrytech Fiber Laser's fiber laser cutting machine has remote monitoring and diagnosis functions. Users can real-time view the operating status of the equipment through mobile phones, computers and other terminal devices, including parameters such as laser power, motor load, cooling water temperature, and gas pressure. In case of equipment failure, the system will immediately automatically alarm and provide users with a detailed fault diagnosis report through data analysis, guiding users to quickly troubleshoot and solve problems. This function greatly shortens the equipment downtime, improves production efficiency, and is especially suitable for production management of large enterprises with wide distribution.
