Precise Beam Transmission and Focusing System

"The laser output from the laser needs to undergo precise transmission and focusing to achieve the best cutting effect on the workpiece, and Senrytech Fiber Laser has demonstrated strong technical strength in this regard.

​ Low-Loss Fiber Transmission: Senrytech Fiber Laser selects high-quality special quartz fiber for laser transmission. This fiber has extremely low transmission loss, with a loss of less than 0.2dB per kilometer. At the same time, the flexible design of the fiber enables it to flexibly adapt to the multi-angle movement of the cutting head, avoiding energy loss and beam deviation caused by improper adjustment of mirrors in traditional optical path systems. When cutting complex three-dimensional workpieces, the fiber can bend freely with the cutting head, ensuring stable transmission of laser energy to the cutting point and guaranteeing the stability and consistency of the cutting process.

​ Intelligent Focusing Cutting Head: The independently developed intelligent focusing cutting head of Senrytech Fiber Laser integrates a number of advanced technologies. Firstly, it adopts a high-precision capacitive height tracking system, which can real-time monitor the height change of the workpiece surface with extremely fast response speed, and can adjust the height of the cutting head in an instant to ensure that the focus is always at the optimal cutting position, with a height control accuracy of up to ±0.01mm. When cutting workpieces with uneven surfaces, this system can effectively avoid cutting quality problems caused by focus deviation. Secondly, the cutting head is equipped with replaceable focusing lenses of various specifications, and users can quickly select lenses with appropriate focal lengths according to the thickness and material of the cutting material to achieve the best focusing effect. For example, when cutting thin plates, short-focus lenses are used to improve cutting speed and precision; when cutting thick plates, long-focus lenses are replaced to enhance the penetration ability of the laser. Thirdly, the cutting head is also integrated with an advanced gas injection system, which can precisely control the flow, pressure and injection angle of the auxiliary gas according to the cutting process requirements. When cutting stainless steel, by precisely adjusting the injection parameters of nitrogen, it can effectively prevent oxidation of the cutting edge and obtain a bright, burr-free cutting surface.
