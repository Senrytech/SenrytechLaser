About Us

Our Story

Jinan Senrytech Machinery Co.. Ltd mainly produces laser cutting machine, welding machine, marking machine,cleaning machine,bending machine and a series of advanced laser equipment.These eguipment integrates the most cutting-edge scientific and technological achievements,and is widely used in various industries with its characteristics of high precision, high efficiency and high stability. Whether it is metal processing, electronic manufacturing,or high-end industries such as automotive,aerospace,the company products can play an excellent performance,to provide customers with high-quality solutions.

As a leading manufacturer of industrial laser equipment, Jinan Senrytech Machinery Co., Ltd. specializes in designing and producing：high-power fiber laser cutting machines, precision laser welding systems, permanent laser marking machines, eco-friendly laser cleaning solutions, and intelligent laser bending centers. Our fiber laser cutting equipment integrates cutting-edge innovations such as AI-driven motion control, IoT-enabled real-time monitoring, and adaptive optics technology, delivering ultra-high precision cutting for metals including stainless steel, carbon steel, aluminum, and copper alloys. With power ranges from 1kW to 40kW, these industrial-grade laser cutters achieve unmatched speed and micron-level accuracy for thin to thick sheet metal (0.5mm–60mm), significantly reducing operating costs while maximizing throughput. Widely adopted in automotive frame fabrication, aerospace component processing, HVAC duct manufacturing, and precision machinery production, Senrytech’s fiber laser cutting solutions offer turnkey automation readi–compatible with robotic arms, auto-loading/unloading systems, and smart factory networks. Choose our CE-certified, energy-efficient laser cutters to transform your metal fabrication workflow with zero-contact processing, minimal maintenance, and industry-leading ROI.


**2001: Rooted in Jinan， Senrytech was established, embarking on the exploration of industrial laser equipment in China.

**2005: The first 500W CO2 laser cutting machine was successfully developed and delivered.

**In 2010: The first 1.2kW fiber laser cutting machine

**In 2013, a 3kW fiber laser cutting machine was independently developed and put into mass production

**In 2018: Breakthrough in 6kW fiber laser cutting technology

**In 2022: Successfully developed a 10,000-watt 12kW and above ultra-high power laser cutting machine
