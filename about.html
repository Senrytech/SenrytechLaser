Whether it is metal processing, electronic manufacturing,or high-end industries such as automotive,aerospace,the company products can play an excellent performance,to provide customers with high-quality solutions.

Senrytech: A Light Blossoming from Adversity​ In the winter of 2008, the cold wind cut through the workshop windows like a knife. Guo Senrui squatted on the icy concrete floor, his gaze fixed on the scattered scrap metal plates, his fingertips trembling uncontrollably. It was an order for precision cutting of aviation components. Each plate was infused with the sweat of the workers, yet the traditional mechanical cutting equipment seemed to be deliberately obstructing their efforts. After seventeen rounds of adjustments, the error remained just beyond the 0.02-millimeter threshold. With only three days left until the delivery deadline, the veteran craftsmen in the workshop gathered around, their brows furrowed in frustration, sighing helplessly: "This job, the old ways just can't handle it anymore."​ That night, Guo Senrui immersed himself in the reference room, his fingertips gliding over stacks of thick journals. When he opened a yellowed laser technology journal, the words "Laser cutting precision can reach 0.01 millimeters" struck him like a bolt of lightning, instantly clearing his muddled thoughts. Memories of his apprenticeship at the machinery factory a decade ago surged into his mind. His master, with calloused hands gripping tools, had earnestly said: "A good tool can make a skilled craftsman even more powerful, but the equipment we have now is like cutting silk with a blunt knife." Those words, like a tiny thorn, had been stuck in his heart for ten years, and now they finally began to ache.​ Without the slightest hesitation, he turned around and listed his wedding-ready house with a real estate agent. When he held the heavy sum of money, the warmth in his palm was almost scalding. This was the first batch of research and development funds he had scraped together. A rented abandoned warehouse became his battlefield. For thirty-seven days and nights, he barely slept, his eyes bloodshot, his body covered in oil stains. He poured all his savings and energy into that cobbled-together experimental device. At the moment of the seventh test run, the laser beam, like a nimble blue lightning, precisely cut a perfect hexagon on the steel plate for the first time. Overwhelmed with excitement, Guo Senrui forgot all his fatigue, suddenly pressing his face against the still-warm plate. The searing heat passed through his cheek, and tears mixed with the oil on his face streamed down, leaving a small smudge on the plate—that blue light was brighter and more dazzling than any light he had ever seen in his life.​ The name "Senrytech" was decided at his hospital bedside. The continuous high-intensity work had drained his strength. When he woke from his drowsiness, sunlight streamed through the gaps in the leaves outside the window, casting a dappled light on the quilt, warm and serene. As he looked at that light pattern, he suddenly remembered what his father had often said when he was alive: the character "Sen" (forest) signifies taking root and growing, doing things steadily like a big tree; while "Rui" (wisdom) represents insight and intelligence, knowing how to adapt and innovate. "Let's call it Senrytech," he turned his head and said to his partner, who was watching over him with a look, "It should be as solid and reliable as a forest, and as precise and sharp as a beam of wisdom."​ On the day the first mass-produced Senrytech laser cutting machine rolled off the production line, the workshop was packed with people. Guo Senrui walked up to the machine and carefully engraved a small line of words on its body: "Let every cutting surface be a work of art." Once, an American client came for an inspection. When he saw the operator control the laser beam to nimbly glide over the metal with just a simple gesture, he curiously asked about the story behind the brand. Guo Senrui smiled and pointed to an old photo on the workshop wall—it was a picture of him and his master in front of an old machine tool, both smiling simple yet bright smiles. "In the past, we relied on our hands; now, we rely on light," he said with full pride, "but our pursuit of precision has never changed."​ Today, Senrytech laser cutting machines have entered factories in more than twenty countries. Every time a new client presses the start button and watches that blue beam dance nimbly over the metal, Guo Senrui can't help but think back to that cold winter night, that life-changing yellowed journal, and his father's earnest teachings about "Sen" and "Rui". This beam of light not only cuts through hard metal but also bridges the gap between the past and the future, allowing that persistent craftsmanship, supported by technology, to go further and more steadily.


**2001: Rooted in Jinan， Senrytech was established, embarking on the exploration of industrial laser equipment in China.
